My Journey into Software Development

By Harshal Prajapati

From Engineering to Building Real-World Web Applications

When I began my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering at Alpha College of Engineering and Technology (2020–2024), I was fascinated by how software could solve real problems. That curiosity became the foundation of my journey into mobile and web development.

During my time in college, I sharpened my fundamentals through coursework while also exploring practical projects. By the time I completed my studies, I had not only learned the theory but also built real applications that I could proudly share.

Professional Experience

In early 2024, I joined Elsner Technologies Pvt. Ltd. as a Flutter Intern. Over six months, I immersed myself in the Flutter SDK and Dart programming language. This experience strengthened my grasp of object-oriented programming (OOP) concepts like encapsulation, inheritance, and polymorphism. Most importantly, I learned how to create cross-platform mobile apps that balance performance with user experience.

Before that, in 2023, I completed a Java program at Samyak Computer Classes, where I built a chatting application using Java, Swing, and AWT. This project gave me insight into how desktop applications handle real-time communication.

Projects That Shaped Me

  • Calculator App (Flutter, Dart): A clean, fast, and efficient cross-platform calculator.
  • E-Library App (Flutter, Dart): A mobile app that lets readers access trending and new-release books on the go.
  • Chatting App (Java, Swing, AWT): A versatile communication platform that provides an interactive chatting experience.

Each of these projects taught me valuable lessons in UI design, performance optimization, and user-focused development.

Skills and Tools

My technical toolkit includes Dart, Java, Android, HTML, CSS, and RESTful APIs. I’m comfortable working with Flutter for app development, and I rely on tools like Android Studio, VS Code, and Eclipse. For databases, I’ve worked with Firebase, Hive, and Shared Preferences.

Looking Ahead

What excites me most about software development is its limitless scope for innovation. Whether it’s building seamless mobile experiences or experimenting with backend systems, I enjoy solving problems and delivering meaningful applications.

As I continue my journey, I aim to contribute to projects that combine creativity, performance, and impact — and to grow as a developer who builds technology for people, not just devices. Right now, I am also learning Web Development to expand my skills even further.
